Headlight Restoration

Restore clarity and improve safety

Why Restore Your Headlights?

Over time, your vehicle's headlights can become cloudy, yellowed, or hazy due to UV exposure, oxidation, and environmental factors. This not only affects the appearance of your car but can significantly reduce visibility and safety while driving at night.

Benefits of Headlight Restoration

  • Improved Visibility - See more clearly at night and in poor weather conditions
  • Enhanced Safety - Better lighting means safer driving for you and others
  • Better Appearance - Crystal clear headlights improve your car's overall look
  • Cost Effective - Much cheaper than replacing headlight assemblies
  • Increased Value - Clean headlights improve resale value

Our Process

  1. Thorough cleaning of headlight surface
  2. Multi-stage wet sanding to remove oxidation
  3. Professional polishing compound application
  4. UV-protective sealant to prevent future yellowing
