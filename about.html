About Us

Family-owned and operated since 1988

Our Story

Uptown Car Wash is a locally and family-owned business established in 1988. For over 35 years, we've been serving the San Diego community with pride, providing drive-through car wash and full-service auto detailing services.

We focus on keeping the exterior and interior of your car looking great. Our team goes the extra mile to exceed customer expectations with every vehicle we service.

State-of-the-Art Equipment

In 2021, we installed brand new state-of-the-art equipment featuring a dual-wash and dry process that combines hand-washing techniques with automated technology. We use only non-caustic, environmentally friendly products to protect both your vehicle and our planet.

Our Services

  • Triple foam polish application
  • Optional ceramic protectant
  • Professional hand-spraying and wiping
  • Interior vacuuming and shampooing
  • Leather cleaning and conditioning
  • Clay bar treatments
  • Upholstery and carpet cleaning

What Sets Us Apart
