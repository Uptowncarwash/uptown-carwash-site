Membership Terms and Conditions

Membership Agreement

By enrolling in the Uptown Car Wash Unlimited Wash Club ("Program"), you agree to the following terms and conditions.

Membership Benefits

  • Unlimited car washes at your selected plan level
  • One vehicle per membership
  • 10% discount on detailing services (select plans)
  • Express Touch discount (select plans)
  • No long-term contract required

Billing

  • Your credit/debit card will be charged on the same date each month
  • First month promotional pricing (50% off) applies to new members only
  • Membership automatically renews each month until cancelled
  • Price changes will be communicated 30 days in advance

Vehicle Requirements

  • Membership is valid for one vehicle only
  • Vehicle must be registered to the membership
  • RFID tag must remain properly attached to windshield
  • Vehicle must fit through our wash tunnel (max height restrictions apply)
  • Membership is non-transferable between vehicles

Vehicle Changes

To change the vehicle on your membership, visit us in person with:

  • Valid photo ID
  • New vehicle registration

One vehicle change per 30-day period is permitted.

Cancellation Policy

  • Cancel anytime with no cancellation fee
  • Cancellation must be requested before your next billing date
  • Cancel in person, by phone, or by email
  • No refunds for partial months
  • Membership benefits continue until end of current billing period

Service Limitations

  • Wash services available during normal business hours only
  • Weather conditions may temporarily close the car wash
  • Membership does not include detailing services (discount may apply)
  • One wash per vehicle per day

RFID Tag Policy

  • RFID tag is property of Uptown Car Wash
  • Lost or damaged tags will be replaced at no charge (first replacement)
  • Subsequent replacements may incur a fee
  • Tags must be returned upon membership cancellation

Liability

Uptown Car Wash is not responsible for:

  • Pre-existing damage to vehicles
  • Loose items, accessories, or aftermarket additions
  • Personal items left in vehicles
