Ceramic Coatings

Ultimate paint protection for your vehicle

What is Ceramic Coating?

Ceramic coating is a liquid polymer that chemically bonds with your vehicle's factory paint, creating a permanent or semi-permanent layer of protection. Unlike traditional wax or sealants that sit on top of the paint, ceramic coating becomes part of your car's surface, providing superior protection and shine.

Benefits of Ceramic Coating

Long-Lasting Protection

Lasts 2-5+ years compared to months with traditional wax

Hydrophobic Properties

Water beads and rolls off, making cleaning easier

UV Protection

Prevents oxidation and fading from sun exposure

Chemical Resistance

Protects against bird droppings, tree sap, and road salt

Enhanced Gloss

Adds depth and shine to your paint's appearance

Scratch Resistance

Adds a harder layer to help prevent light scratches
